About

This directory page is designed as a large classified listing hub with categories, featured placements, promoted cards and banner advertising blocks. The structure is ideal for SEO landing pages with a lot of directory content.

Each profile card can represent a listing, company, agency or business page. Paid status can move cards higher in the grid, while banners can be shown in fixed positions on the right side and in hero sections above the catalog.

The filter area supports category sorting, custom attributes, premium marks, quick tags and checkbox-style flags. This allows you to build dynamic archive pages and large city-based directories with custom ranking logic.

You can connect WordPress custom post types, ACF fields, taxonomies and your own promotion system to control top positions, active packages, banner visibility and featured labels.

The lower text section works as a strong SEO block for category descriptions, city descriptions, internal links and AI-generated paragraphs. It is also suitable for FAQ snippets, trust texts and long-form content.

This layout is especially useful for projects where the main goal is directory visibility, monetized placements, banner sales and content scaling across many categories and geo pages.
